Invest in a membership level that fits your needs and budget. The more you use BarkingLot, the more you save!

  • Non-Members: Each visit requires verification of vaccination status and completion of a temperament and behavior questionnaire for your dog. This is to ensure safety and compatibility with our services.
  • (Free) Basic Membership: Enjoy a streamlined drop-off process with your pet's records saved in our system. No need to repeatedly provide vaccination status or fill out questionnaires about your dog's temperament and behavior at each visit.
  • ($40/month) Silver Membership: Pay for your Silver membership and receive an equal amount of free service hours. Plus, enjoy a 10% discount on all additional hours beyond your free time.
  • ($80/month) Gold Membership: With Gold membership, get free service hours equal to your membership cost, and enjoy a 15% discount on all extra hours beyond your free time.
  • ($160/mont) Platinum Membership: Our Platinum membership offers free service hours equal to your membership price, plus an exclusive 20% discount on all extra hours beyond your free time.

For more information or to request a membership, contact us at memberships@barkinglot.app
